Mobile Agro Processing

Bringing Advanced Agro-Processing to Your Doorstep

Imagine having a fully equipped, state of the art processing facility drive right up to your farm or market. Gadjama Innovation makes this a reality with our revolutionary mobile agro-processing unit. This all-in-one truck is designed to provide farmers, butchers, and small-scale agribusinesses with immediate access to professional-grade services, transforming fresh produce into value-added products on the spot. By eliminating the need for expensive fixed infrastructure and transportation to distant processing plants, we empower your business, enhance food safety, and ensure the highest quality and freshness for your customer

Meat Cutter

Our professional meat cutting service ensures that your livestock is processed with precision and expertise. Using clean, sharp tools and proper techniques, we can break down carcasses into precise cuts of meat—from prime steaks and roasts to stewing cubes and minced meat—tailored to your specific market demands. This service adds significant value to your product, making it more appealing and ready for direct sale to consumers, restaurants, and retailers.

Chicken Cleaner

Hygiene and efficiency are paramount in poultry processing. Our chicken cleaning service handles the entire process, from slaughter to packaging, in a controlled and sanitary environment within the mobile unit. We ensure that each bird is thoroughly cleaned, defeathered, and eviscerated according to strict health standards. This guarantees a safe, presentable, and high-quality final product that builds trust with your customers and extends the shelf life of your poultry.

Vacuum Sealer

Preservation is key to reducing waste and maximizing profitability. Our on-board vacuum sealer removes air from specially designed bags before sealing them tightly. This process dramatically extends the freshness of meat, poultry, and even dehydrated goods by preventing freezer burn and inhibiting the growth of spoilage bacteria. Vacuum-sealed products have a much longer shelf life, maintain their juiciness and flavor, and present a professional, market-ready appearance that is ideal for storage and sale.
